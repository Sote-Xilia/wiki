Virtual Assessment

Virtual Assessments

Many doctors, when first learning about Praxis, do not grasp the concept that you never start with the history. In fact, that sound wrong! What if you don't know at first what is wrong with your patient? Clearly, you may not have a diagnosis at first, or ever! But, as you will learn, you always have an assessment, always, unless, of course, you are a medical student. Then you may not have an assessment, but of course, you cannot practice medicine either. You will find that the Praxis assessment is a lot more than simply creating full cases with diagnoses and all. They really a shorthand for your mind. For example, "fever" may generate a fantastic checklist history of fever, or headache, or abdominal pain. In addition, they will help you bring "clusters" of laboratories or medications you tend to order together. The next chapters begin to close those gaps in the initial training. Still, no chronic cases until the end of basic training!
